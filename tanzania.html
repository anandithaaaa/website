Intermediary Supported Information Access for Farmers in Tanzania

This research investigates how intermediaries — agricultural extension workers and community-based facilitators — can bridge the information gap between smallholder farmers and digital advisory systems in Tanzania. The work examines how technology can be designed to work through, rather than around, these trusted human channels.
