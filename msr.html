LLM Enabled, Chatbot Delivered Care in Southern India

This project explores the design and deployment of large language model-powered chatbots to deliver healthcare information and support in Southern India. The research examines how conversational AI can be adapted to local languages, cultural contexts, and low-resource settings to meaningfully extend access to care.
